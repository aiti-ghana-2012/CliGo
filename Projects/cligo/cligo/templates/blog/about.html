    About The Team

    Afiday

    Team


    Our commitment is to provide innovative solutions for the African Community


    Afiday is a company that sprung out of the MIT-AITI initiative. It specifically began in July 2012. The name "Afiday" is a refined name to the Twi meaning of the word "machine" which is "afidie." The reason behind the choice is that machines are efficient in doing work and in making our lifes easy and more productive.
    As a result, we, at Afiday, are poised at making a change in the lives of ordinary people by providing simple mobile solutions that helps makes lives better. A poster child to this claude is CliGo, which provides periodic health tips, and very soon appointments, to pregnant women in Ghana.
